About me

Because of this I’m majoring in economics with a math emphasis and intend to also double major in data science. Currently a rising junior at UW Madison, I plan to pursue a career where I can contribute data-driven solutions to business decision-making. I’m presently cementing my skillset self-studying programming languages like python, R...and honing those skills with hands-on opportunities to apply what I have learned. I also take great interest in exploring research opportunities in fields needing the application of data science. “Learning by doing”, is my motto! Being involved in a community is important to me and I have been involved in a wide range of activities at UW-Madison from being a student org board member to being a psychological support volunteer. I am excited to continue to grow by contributing my skills to your organization.
